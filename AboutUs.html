We are a Store that cares about domestic animals. Be it grooming health checks, toys,food or simply just babysitting.
We were founded in 2021 by our majestic owner Feline Dogsworth who was a former veterinarian. She owned 3 dogs, 5 cats, 7 birds and 8 hamsters.
Our mission, out aim is to make sure both pets and owners are left happy and looking forward to the next visit.
